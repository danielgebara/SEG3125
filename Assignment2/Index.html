Our Story

About Maison Dorée

Maison Dorée was founded in 2018 by head stylist Sophie Laurent with one goal: to create a salon where every client feels genuinely cared for. We are a small, experienced team of five stylists, each specializing in different areas of hair care.

We believe that a good haircut starts with listening. Every appointment begins with a consultation so we understand exactly what you are looking for before we pick up the scissors.

Walk-ins are welcome, but we recommend booking ahead to guarantee your preferred stylist and time.

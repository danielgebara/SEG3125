About

Maison Dorée was founded in 2018 by head stylist Sophie Laurent. We are a team of five stylists, each specializing in different areas of hair care.

Every appointment starts with a consultation so we understand exactly what you are looking for before we start. We have been serving Ottawa clients for over 7 years and have a 4.9 rating on Google.
